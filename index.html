EGGONOMY

A Supply Chain Simulation

Which came first — the chicken or the profit?

HOW TO PLAY

📦

1. SOURCE

Each round, decide how many eggs to buy from suppliers. Balance cost, speed, and risk.

🏭

2. PRODUCE

Hatch eggs into chickens or sell them directly. Chickens produce eggs or meat over time.

🧊

3. STORE

Manage your inventory carefully. Eggs spoil after a few rounds. Overstock wastes money.

🚚

4. DISTRIBUTE

Choose fast or slow delivery. Local or global sourcing. Each has cost/risk tradeoffs.

👥

5. SATISFY

Meet customer demand each round. Satisfaction drives your final score and reputation.

🏆

WIN CONDITION

Complete 10 rounds with a final score of ≥ 2000 points and customer satisfaction ≥ 60%.
